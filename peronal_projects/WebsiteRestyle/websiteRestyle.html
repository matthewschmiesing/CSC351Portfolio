Bad Website Restyling

Homiletic and Pastoral Review Website Improvements

What's Wrong?

-When first opening the page, you are not drawn to the content, rather you are drawn to the intense green background.

-The small content headers do not bring any attention to themselves.

-There is almost more background dead space than there is content space.

What's Better?

-The light green background brings less attention to itself.

-The larger headers bring more attention to the content of the page.

-The wider main content section moves the page closer to following the 60/30/10 rule and brings more attention to the main content.

*More improvements are definitely needed.
